My Life

My Journey

I started programming as a simple hobby, but soon enough it landed me a degree at the prestigious University of Oxford, and jobs at the biggest tech giants worldwide, including Microsoft and Google. Now, I'm looking for a side job to teach others the art of determination and life management.


Early Life

As a determined student, I attended highly competitive schools in the City of London, those of which include Eton College, and St. Olave's Grammar School, for sixth form; proving the power of focused and positive attitude even from a young age. I left school with A*s in Maths, Further Maths and Computer Science, and A in Economics.


Careers

Having started a simple life in Cambridgeshire, I decided to move out and explore what the tech world had to offer. With a straight face and a stroke of luck, I landed myself an office job at Microsoft. Since then, I worked my way up the ranks and currently am self-employed and earning 6 figures.
